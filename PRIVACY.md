# Privacy Policy for Does LLM Fit

Effective date: 2026-03-28

Does LLM Fit ("the app") is designed to work with model metadata and hardware inputs you provide on your device.

## What data the app collects

The app does not require account sign-in and does not collect personal identity data such as name, email, phone number, or payment information.

## What data is processed on your device

The app processes the following locally on your device:

- Hardware values you enter or select (for example GPU, VRAM, RAM, and context length).
- Model catalog data packaged with the app.
- App state needed for normal runtime behavior.

## Network usage

The app may request public image and metadata resources used to display model organization logos and catalog content. These requests are made to public endpoints (for example, Hugging Face-hosted resources).

The app does not transmit your personal files or user-entered free-form content to backend services operated by the developer.

## Analytics and tracking

The app does not include third-party analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or behavioral tracking services.

## Data sharing

The developer does not sell or share personal data because the app does not collect personal data from users.

## Data retention

Because personal data is not collected by the developer, there is no developer-side personal data retention.

## Children

The app is not directed to children under 13, and no known personal data collection is performed.

## Security

Reasonable technical safeguards are used for app distribution and signing. No system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

## Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted in this repository and take effect on the listed effective date.
